About Global Classes

Located in Semra, East Champaran, Bihar, Global Classes is a growing educational institute known for its blend of traditional and modern teaching methods.

We provide specialized classes for 8th to 12th standard students and also guide learners preparing for competitive exams like ITI, Polytechnic, GK, and more.

Our facilities include YouTube-based online classes, PDF and handwritten notes, English and Hindi medium instruction, purified water, Wi-Fi, library access, and hygienic washrooms.
